Kazimir Malevich, 1915

Painterly Realism of a Football Player

Kazimir Severinovich Malevich is the founder of Suprematism. He has participated in exhibitions of the "Jack of Diamonds" group, "Salon des Indépendants" in Paris, and in 1915 at the "Last Futurist Exhibition of Paintings 0,10" and exhibited 36 works. Black Square - the main work of the artist.
